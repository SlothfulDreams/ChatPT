Physical Therapy is Broken
Patients struggle with generic exercise plans, lack of real-time feedback, and no way to visualize their recovery progress.

Expensive Access

🤷

No Visibility

02 — Our Solution
An AI Physiotherapist in Your Browser
ChatPT combines an interactive 3D body model, a voice-enabled AI chat, and evidence-based workout generation—all in one platform.
  • Voice-Enabled AI Chat: Speak naturally about your symptoms. Whisper transcribes your voice, and the AI auto-selects affected muscles on the 3D model.
  • 3D Body Visualization: 100+ individually selectable muscle meshes with real-time condition coloring. The AI highlights muscles as you describe pain.
  • RAG Knowledge Base: Evidence-based retrieval from clinical PT literature. Every recommendation is grounded in real research.
  • Chat-to-Workout: Ask for a workout in chat and it's instantly created in your workout plans. The AI respects your injuries and adapts to your goals.

04 — Workout Generation
AI-Personalized Exercise Plans
Generate workouts from chat or the dedicated panel. The AI avoids loading injured muscles and adapts to your equipment.

Two Ways to Generate

From Chat

"Give me a shoulder rehab workout"—the AI calls create_workout() and the plan instantly appears in your workout panel.

From Workout Panel

Configure goals, duration (15-180 min), equipment, and focus areas. The AI generates exercises with DALL-E images.

Smart Safety

  • Injury-Aware: Avoids loading torn/strained muscles
  • Progressive: Lighter loads for recovering muscles
  • Evidence-Based: Sourced from RAG clinical protocols
  • Reasoned: Includes clinical reasoning notes
  • Validated: Muscle targets verified against mesh IDs
